Testing

Our dedicated test team

Our test team ensure all of our sites are fully functional in the latest browser and mobile versions. Testing is fully baked into our development process to ensure that all work is design compliant, fully responsive, accessible, usable and beautiful.

We cover the latest versions of chrome, firefox, safari and our device testing suite checks the sites on the most up to date screen sizes and operating systems.

Our team live to root out bugs in the front-end code; they work iteratively with our developers, and their diligence and attention to detail help us to maintain exceptionally high standards.

Our processes are set up to help you get the very best out of your project. We have carefully developed how we test to enable us to deliver large enterprise software solutions that work across the most common devices and all screen sizes and have AA compliant templates.

Heather Stroud, Test Team Lead
