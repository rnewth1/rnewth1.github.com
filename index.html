About

I graduated from De Montfort University in 2013 with a BSc (hons) Computing degree.

Since 2011, I have been a Web Designer/Developer at ThinkOn Global Ltd. I'm responsible for the design, front-end development and maintenance of our digital products. Additionally, I create marketing campaigns, various print work and am a general all-round helping hand.

I like to bring a creative, fresh approach to all of my work and am passionate about user experience and modern technologies.

Whilst away from the screen, you can find me enjoying a theatre performance, playing the occasional video game or doing a spot of DIY. Otherwise, I will most likely be watching an animated movie or binging on the latest reality TV programme!
